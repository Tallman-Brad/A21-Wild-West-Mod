====================================
= Chaz916Gaming Wild West POI List =
====================================
Chaz916Gaming kindly submitted 29 POI candidates for the Wild West Mod.
These POIs range from custom made POIs to TFP conversions.
They displayed a multitude of biome.xml spawning assets both above and below ground which infers they were directly exported from a World.

As such, making them compatible with regular POI features required some intensive updating as follows:
- Removal of biome placed underground ore from the terrain blocks which were very noticable in cross section export cuts
- Removal of deco spawning topsoil assets
- Replacing all terrain to Terrain Filler to match the biome
- Replacing player assets such as player lights (torches, lanterns, and candles), as well as writeable crates and player crates, among others.

The Wild West Mod team working on POIs (Medusa, Tallman Brad, and arramus) made extensive overhaul updates, but maintained as much of the original context as possible.
They all conform with default POI standard.
TMB Gameplay Community appreciate this assortment of custom made and repurposed TFP repurposed POIs shared by Chaz916Gaming.
